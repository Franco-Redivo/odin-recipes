Chocolate chip cookie

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chip

Steps

  • Beat the butter and sugar,then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  • Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixter.
  • Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  • Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  • Bake until the edges are golden brown.
